SafeHands TVET Initiative

A Blaque Orchids Group CSR Initiative equipping South Africa's next generation of artisans, civil engineers, and safety officers with commercial-grade Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).

Donate

Our Mission

At Blaque Orchids Group, we believe that you cannot build a sustainable nation without securing its foundational workforce. Across South Africa's rural Technical and Vocational Education and Training (TVET) colleges, thousands of underprivileged students enter workshops, civil construction yards, and electrical hubs daily without standard Personal Protective Equipment (PPE).

Project Prime bridges this safety gap. Alongside our strategic partners, we donate premium, compliant industrial safety gear—including steel-toe boots, high-visibility reflector vests, protective eyewear, and safety helmets—directly to rural TVET students. We are eliminating barriers to entry, ensuring workplace dignity, and protecting the future human commodity of the built environment.

Partner With Us: Secure the Future Workforce

To our peers across the South African civil and construction sectors: Infrastructure deployment relies on the safety and training of the hands that build it. We invite established construction companies, materials suppliers, and plant hire firms to join hands with Project Prime through PPE asset donations or corporate sponsorship.

Your contribution does not just protect a student—it strengthens your corporate standing. All strategic corporate donations qualify for comprehensive Section 18A Tax Certificates, direct B-BBEE Socio-Economic Development (SED) points, and prominent branding across our national TVET deployment media campaigns. Let us build South Africa responsibly, from the ground up.
