About Us

Established in 2015, Blaque Orchids Group has evolved over more than a decade into a diversified, Level 1 B-BBEE tri-sector industrial powerhouse operating as Specialist Contractors for the Built Environment across South Africa. Grounded in the absolute principle that human capital is the nation's primary commodity, we engineer, maintain, and safeguard critical infrastructure and commercial property assets through an uncompromised, vertically integrated corporate framework.
