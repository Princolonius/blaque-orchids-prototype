About Us

Established in 2016, Blaque Orchids Group Pty Ltd has remained true to its character name by growing from strength to strength in the midst of changing and challenging times over the years, and remains true and steadfast to its course through dedication and perseverance. The year is 2024 and today! Blaque Orchids Group has transitioned and matured from a single service business into a Multidivisional group of companies with 3 priority divisions, Construction, Logistics & Retail and Security & Cleaning.

Our priority divisions offer integrated services to ensure a seamless customer service and experience, and each division seeks to operate within our values and principles which include, Quality, Value for Money, Honest and Transparent, Reliable and Consistent.

Rhuma

Logistics and Retail:

Our vibrant and energized commodities logistics company RHUMA logistics, loosely translated in setswana language meaning “To send” seeks to participate, disrupt and emerge victorious in the logistics industry by building reputability, reliability with a consideration for value for money through our services. We combine our logistics with the latest security technology and route management to ensure your commodities leave the depot and reach your site on time, every time! Thrive Risk & Security

Risk and Security:

We are a Risk & Security company combining human capital, technology and security workforce for the purpose of maintaining order, safety and peace. Our core service includes mitigating physical risk in residential complexes, commercial buildings, education institutions, work environments and places of high risk.
