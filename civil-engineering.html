Our Services

Focus: Heavy-duty public infrastructure, road network logistics, and earthworks.

Routine Road Maintenance (RRM)

Pothole patch development, longitudinal crack sealing, asphalt rejuvenation, edge break repairs, and edge-line vegetation control.

Asphalt Surfacing & Rehabilitation

Deep-mill surfacing, layout of hot-mix asphalt layers, slurry mix applications, Cape seals, and chip-and-spray road surfacing.

Bulk Earthworks & Site Preparation

Heavy land clearing, structural subgrade excavation, soil stabilization, compaction testing, and base layer leveling.

Stormwater & Wet Infrastructure

Reinforced concrete culvert placement, open-channel drainage lining, stone pitching, subsurface drainage pipelines, and erosion control barrier installations.

Road Safety Infrastructure

High-visibility permanent road marking (thermoplastic application), regulatory road sign erection, guardrail installations, and raised pavement markers (cat-eyes).
